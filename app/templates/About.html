About

Dataexp is an application for exploring and visualizing the timeseries. It works with MySQL and Mongo databases, and can also load datasets from CSV or JSON files. The application offers the possibility to visualize dynamic data-based graphs, cutting the timseries, interpolating the timeseries, correlation detection and size reduction.

The application is Node.js based using Angular-Material for the front-end. Developped by Mykhailo Ganza with supervision of François Naçabal at Maya Technologies.
