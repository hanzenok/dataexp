clearAll( )
app/js/controllers/DeleteZoneCtrl.js:103

A local scope method that fires when the delete zone (placed in the footer of index.html) is clicked.
It clears all the Drag&Drop objects from their respective lists. It also deletes a loaded dataset.

app/js/controllers/DeleteZoneCtrl.js:16

A local scope method tha removes a dropped object from the list of objects of its type.
Dropped objects could be:

  • normal fields
  • timestamp fields
  • DC.js or Canvas.js charts
  • movable charts
